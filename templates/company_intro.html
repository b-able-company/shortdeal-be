About B.able Company

B.able is a Korea-based short-form drama IP management company specializing in content planning, production, and global distribution.
The company works closely with a wide range of Korean production studios through strategic partnerships, covering the entire value chain from early-stage development to international platform distribution.

With a strong portfolio of Korean short-form drama IPs and script assets, B.able offers flexible collaboration models including content licensing, co-production, and distribution partnerships, tailored to the needs of different platforms and markets. B.able is committed to expanding the global reach and commercial potential of Korean short-form drama content.
